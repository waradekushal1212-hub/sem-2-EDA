## NLP Techniques Implemented

### 1. Tokenization

Tokenization is the process of splitting text into smaller units such as words or sentences.

* Word Tokenization
* Sentence Tokenization

---

### 2. Stopword Removal

Stopwords are common words such as is, the, a, an that do not add much meaning.
These words are removed to improve processing efficiency.

---

### 3. Stemming

Stemming reduces words to their root form by removing suffixes.
Example: running → run
It may not always produce meaningful words.

---

### 4. Lemmatization

Lemmatization converts words to their base or dictionary form.
Example: running → run
It is more accurate than stemming.

---

### 5. Part-of-Speech (POS) Tagging

POS tagging assigns grammatical labels to words such as:

* Noun (NN)
* Verb (VB)
* Adjective (JJ)

---

### 6. Frequency Distribution

It counts how often each word appears in the text.
This helps in identifying important words.
